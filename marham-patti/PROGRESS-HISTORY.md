### Problem

Without a first-class flag engine in the foundation, every later module would hard-code "the clinic has a lab" and break the moment a pharmacy-only tenant is sold. The engine must exist BEFORE any feature module so all of them gate cleanly, and it must gate BEFORE RBAC (a disabled capability is invisible + forbidden regardless of role).
