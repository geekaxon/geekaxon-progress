## Build-step 21 вЂ” Phase 1: Billing & Cashier

**Status:** вњ… DONE/APPROVED (2026-07-04) В· **Branch:** `feat/21-billing-cashier` (WORK TYPE: FEATURE) В· **Spec:** `specs/21-billing-cashier.md`

**What shipped.** ONE billing surface across OPD (17), lab (19) and pharmacy (20) вЂ” the founding "cash is where money leaks" pain:
- **Unified invoice** built from an **enabled source** and adapting to the tenant's flags: `CONSULT` (`clinic.consultation`) / `LAB` + `PACKAGE` (`lab.results`) / `PHARMACY` (`pharmacy.pos`) / `OTHER` (manual line). A **disabled source is rejected (400)** вЂ” a pharmacy-only tenant can't bill a lab order (Acceptance В§1). Lines are **auto-pulled read-only** from the source document (a pharmacy sale's items, a lab order's tests joined to catalog prices, a package's price) so 17/19/20 are never modified; `CONSULT` keys the fee as `amount` because the consult domain stores none. `computeInvoiceTotals` clamps the discount to `[0, subtotal]` and applies tax on the discounted base.
- **Payments** (cash/card/online/**credit=udhaar**): partial allowed в†’ `PARTIAL` + pending balance, `paid в‰Ґ total` в†’ `PAID`. A **CREDIT** payment posts a **`PatientCredit` entry (14)** via the existing `PatientService.addCredit` **and** an **AR accounting event**; a credit payment with no patient в†’ 400.
- **Refunds**: a **reason-code is required** (audited). Over a threshold the refund lands **`REQUESTED`** and only a **`billing.refund.approve`** holder can approve/reject before the money moves; at/under it clears at once. The **refundable amount subtracts already-claimed refunds** (APPROVED reduced `paid`; REQUESTED are pending claims) so an invoice can **never be over-refunded**, and `settleRefund` re-validates against the CURRENT `paid`. A **partial** refund reopens the balance (`PARTIAL`/`PAID`) so remaining money stays collectable; only a **full** refund в†’ `REFUNDED`.
- **Cashier shift**: open (float) в†’ collect в†’ **close with a denomination counter в†’ variance = counted в€’ (float + cash in в€’ cash out)** вЂ” the owner's **theft tripwire** (kept exact in pure math). The refund cash-out is charged to the **requesting** cashier's shift (captured at request time), not the approver's, so the variance lands on the right person.
- **End-of-day per-person report**: collections by method, refunds and variance per cashier (`collected` excludes udhaar вЂ” a receivable, not cash in hand).
- **Receipts**: thermal 80mm + A4 self-printing windows with the 07 letterhead.
- **Accounting events в†’ 23**: each settle/refund writes ONE **idempotent balanced double-entry** `AccountingEvent` (ОЈdebit = ОЈcredit via `assertBalanced`) into an outbox `@@unique(tenant, source, sourceRefId)`; a re-emit is a no-op (`createMany skipDuplicates`). `pay` / `refund` / shift-`close` all ride **`SyncService.runIdempotent`** (Idempotency-Key) so an offline replay never double-charges / double-refunds / double-posts / double-credits.

- **Alerts:** **stock-loss value** (ОЈ |qtyDelta|Г—cost over DAMAGE/EXPIRE/negative-ADJUST movements вЂ” the founding pain surfaced to the owner), **low-stock** (on-hand в‰¤ threshold 10), **cashier shift variance** (non-zero closed-shift variance вЂ” the theft tripwire), **overdue udhaar/AR** (open invoices в‰Ґ 30 days), **pending lab approvals** (RESULTED orders), **audit highlights** (sensitive action-prefixes: refunds/voids/period-close/opening/returns/amendments/payouts/roles/flags), **expiry risk** (stub `available:false` until 28 ships inventory batches вЂ” the card shows for pharmacy so the shape is stable).

**Flag adaptation (the load-bearing rule).** Each card declares the flag(s) that make it relevant (ANY-of; empty = always) + an optional sensitive permission. `visibleCards(flags, perms, section)` filters: a **pharmacy-only** tenant sees sales/stock-loss/low-stock/expiry/top-medicines but NO lab or doctor cards; a **lab-only** tenant sees TAT/volume/pending-approvals but no pharmacy/doctor cards; a **clinic** tenant sees footfall/doctors+commission.
